# Probably Too Dangerous

These are wild directions I would not prioritize early even in a moonshot pass.

## Validation-Like Facts Hidden in an Artifact Memory

Even if technically paid for in bytes, this is close enough to “memorization of the target distribution” that it would attract scrutiny and likely become a distraction.

## Full Tokenizer Redesign Before Solving the Existing Bottlenecks

The current evidence points more strongly to optimization and export mismatch than to tokenizer bottlenecks. Tokenizer work is legitimate, but it is not my first wild bet.

## Exotic Bit-Level Weight Formats Without Training Built Around Them

If the whole training/export story is not designed around the representation, this becomes a high-complexity way to degrade the submitted model.
