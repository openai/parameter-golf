```

legitimacy: likely-illegal
claimed_bpb: 1.05970
builds_on: #1530, #1626

```

dexhunter's **intermediate submission** predating #1736. Uses lossy `.lower()`
casefold applied to all training + validation data before SP8192 BPE. The BPB
denominator is derived from casefolded piece UTF-8 bytes, not original bytes —
systematically deflating the denominator relative to the competition metric.
Issue #1604 explicitly covers this pattern. The 0.0058 bpb gap vs #1736
(1.05970 vs 1.06549) is almost certainly casefold artifact. **dexhunter replaced
this with lossless CaseOps in #1736** — our baseline already incorporates the
legal version of this idea. No novel extractable lever.

SLOT-4 is a per-window logit-bias delta optimizer: optimize delta on `y[:wlen]`,

scored-position SLOT pattern confirmed non-causal in Issue #1336/PR #1240 (100%

our baseline**. The claimed 1.0616 is entirely SLOT-derived and would evaporate
under a legality ruling. No novel training-time lever. Lossless SP8192, no
`from fla.` — only SLOT is the issue.
