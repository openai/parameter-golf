## Key Findings

1. **Adjacent band peaks at vocab 2048-3072 (~54%)** then drops steadily. At 16384 it reaches 37.6%.
2. **Long-range bands show a U-curve:** 22.8% at V=512, dip to 18.3% at V=2048, rise to 31.5% at V=16384.
3. **Sweet spot: vocab 8192 / dim 320 / 20 layers** — 12.7% embedding, 43.6% adjacent, 28.0% long-range.
4. **Beyond 8192:** entropy continues redistributing but embedding cost grows too fast (25%+ at V=16384).
5. **Baseline (V=1024/dim=512/8 layers)** has only 2.7% embedding — severely underweight vs 10-15% optimal.
