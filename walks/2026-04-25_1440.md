The recurrent mode currently has `attn_scale`, `mlp_scale`, `resid_mix` shared across all L invocations of the same block. The model can't differentiate "same block called 3 times on different states." This is exactly what Universal Transformers / Relaxed Recursive Transformers solve via per-loop adaptation. **Adding per-loop scalars is ~15 lines and could be a real win.** L=3 × per-block 1024 floats = ~9K params per block × 3 = ~27K extra params. Negligible cost, unlocks compute differentiation per loop.

GPTQ is also tempting but multi-hundred lines and we can't modify the eval quantize function. We'd have to bake calibrated permutations/scalings into the weights themselves. Park as late-session item.
